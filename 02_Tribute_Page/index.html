Steve Jobs

Remembering a Visionary

☛ Steve Jobs was born in San Francisco, California, on February 24, 1955.

☛ He co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne in 1976, revolutionizing the technology industry.

☛ Jobs was known for his visionary leadership, contributing to the development of iconic products like the Macintosh, iPhone, and iPad.

☛ He was ousted from Apple in 1985 but returned in 1997, leading the company to new heights and introducing innovative products.

☛ Steve Jobs' famous Stanford commencement speech in 2005 continues to inspire people worldwide.

☛ He passed away on October 5, 2011, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and design excellence.

☛ Jobs' motto "Think Different" reflects his approach to life and work, emphasizing creativity and individuality.
